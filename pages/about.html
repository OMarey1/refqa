Our Values

We believe in full participation

People with disabilities are an essential part of the student community. Their natural place is to participate side by side with everyone.

Inclusion First

Everyone is welcome to participate side by side, with equal opportunity and respect.

Empowerment through Action

Real empowerment comes from practice, experience, and giving actual roles and responsibilities.

Learning Together

Every activity is a chance for mutual learning: students with disabilities learn, and others learn from them.

Respect & Dignity

We reject pity and emphasize treating everyone with dignity and respect.

Collaboration over Competition

Our success comes from working together as one team, complementing each other.

Sustainability of Impact

We aim for a lasting impact, creating a permanent shift in how the community thinks.

Our Story

The Rafqa Journey

Rafqa started as a spark from a real story a determined person overlooked by society. Through teamwork and belief, we turned the idea into a platform for inclusion, support, and real impact.

From a hackathon dream to second place, our mission lives on building a world of exchange, not pity.
